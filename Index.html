🤝 Zurich Classic V2 — Team Format + Dual Snapshot

The only team event on Tour. 74 two-man teams · 4 rounds · alternate shot (R1/R3) and best ball (R2/R4). DraftKings: 4-team lineups under $50k. V2 adds live odds import, S1→S2 shift tracking, and steam detection for teams.

📸 Dual-Snapshot Delta

Paste early open odds as S1, then Wed closing lines as S2 in the DATA tab. The table shows the exact implied probability shift — sustained shortening = sharp money on that team.

🔄 Format Matters

Alternate shot rewards chemistry + ball-striking consistency. Best ball rewards upside. Target teams with complementary games — one bomber + one wedge/putter specialist is the ideal alt-shot pairing.

⚡ Value Signal

Positive Δ = DK underpriced this team vs. the betting market. With only 4 roster spots, pricing inefficiencies matter even more than in individual events. One mispriced team swings the whole lineup.

🚀 Optimizer

GPP mode maximizes composite score + leverage. Δ/Fit mode maximizes market value × course fit. Lock teams first, then auto-fill remaining slots. Knapsack DP under $50k cap.

💡 TPC Louisiana Course Notes

✅ WHAT WINS HERE

Length off the tee — TPC Louisiana rewards bombers. Par 5s are reachable and scoreable.

SG: Approach — Soft Bermuda greens hold well. Ball-strikers thrive.

Best Ball ceiling — GPP lineups are won in R2 and R4. Target teams where BOTH players have birdie-or-better upside, not just one carrying the other.

Alternate shot chemistry — Teams with proven partnership history or natural rhythm match (both aggressive or both conservative) outperform scratch pairings in R1/R3.

⚠️ OWNERSHIP TRAPS

Clark/Moore and Koepka/Lowry will likely be 30%+ owned each. Fine to include, but both in the same lineup eliminates GPP edge entirely.

Late withdrawal risk — Team events have higher WD risk since both players must be healthy. Check injury reports at lock.

Untested pairings (pos 40-74) — Many of these teams are playing together for the first time. Alt shot variance is real — a single bad hole wrecks both scores.
